# Code Of Conduct

This project welcomes technical criticism, failed replications, negative
results, and competing methods. Participation must remain respectful and
focused on evidence.

## Expected Conduct

- Critique claims and methods without attacking people.
- Disclose limitations, conflicts of interest, and material uncertainty.
- Do not manipulate, selectively omit, or misrepresent experimental evidence.
- Respect privacy and do not publish another person's credentials or private data.
- Give credit for code, analysis, data, and conceptual contributions.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, and deliberate disruption are not
acceptable. Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainer using the email
in the package metadata. Reports will be reviewed confidentially and may result
in warnings, contribution restrictions, or removal from project spaces.
